Did you Know...

Black Tea

Strong in flavour versatile and a general favourite. Black tea is what comes to mind when we think of tea. Black tea doesn’t grow any differently to green this is a common misconception. Black tea is green tea… well with a few extra steps.

Green Tea

Green tea is largely unprocessed and is thee most traditional way to drink tea. Well known for its more mellow scent and distinctly earthy flavour and slightly bitter taste. Green tea is often considered to be amongst the most healthy of tea options.

Red Tea

Rooibos tea or Red tea not, not to be confused with Chinese red tea which is black tea. Rooibos tea is made from special tea plants indigenous to south Africa. Among the reasons for Rooibos is its lack of caffeine and tannins which is said to cause the bitter tase in green and black teas.
